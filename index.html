ABOUT

Upon returning home from the "The Big One" in the late 40's, Stephen developed a passion for storytelling and filmmaking. An avid writer and Scattergories aficionado, Stephen spends most of his free time writing scripts and developing new projects. Additionally, he posseses what the French call, L'oeil, or "The Eye", for photography which has been developed from decades of watching movies and Models Inc.

Stephen also posseses the knowledge of the universe as he is a Ph.D chemist with two publications to his name - whoa, someone call the nerd police! But, don't let that scare you - he doesn't take himself too seriously and only wants to use that prefix if it means better marketing to make movies.

With all that self-aggrandizing horse shit out of the way, Stephen wants to make challenging, experimental and most of all, interesting movies that leave the spectator wanting answers, or their money back. Take a chance on this semi-overweight and "intelligent" filmmaker - ABBA would if they could.
